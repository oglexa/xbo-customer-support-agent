# ApexCrypto Security & Compliance: Account Freeze Policy

ApexCrypto balances user convenience with absolute security and legal compliance. Under specific circumstances, the platform's Risk Management or Legal Affairs departments may institute a temporary or permanent freeze on an account's trading, deposit, or withdrawal functionalities.

### Primary Triggers for Account Freezes

1. **Security-Initiated Freeze (User or System Requested):**
   * **Credential Modifications:** As cross-referenced in our *Withdrawal Limits Framework*, any change to primary security settings (password reset, disabling 2FA, or modifying the registered email) triggers an automatic, non-negotiable 48-hour withdrawal freeze.
   * **Self-Lockout:** If a user clicks the "Freeze Account" link embedded in any login notification email, the account is immediately locked to prevent suspected unauthorized access.
2. **Compliance and Fraud-Initiated Freeze:**
   * **Suspicious Activity Flag:** If an account breaches the behavioral thresholds monitored by our security team (as outlined in *Suspicious Activity Monitoring*), a 24-hour diagnostic freeze is initiated.
   * **Failed Re-Verification:** If a user’s verification document expires or is flagged during periodic audits, the account is frozen at Tier 1 restrictions until updated compliance data is submitted.
3. **Regulatory and Legal Mandates:**
   * **Law Enforcement Requests:** ApexCrypto complies with valid court orders, subpoenas, and directives from authorized international law enforcement agencies. In such scenarios, accounts are frozen immediately without prior notification to the account holder.

### Types of Account Restrictions
* **Partial Freeze:** The user can log in and trade or deposit assets, but outbound fiat and cryptocurrency withdrawals are completely blocked.
* **Full Freeze:** The user is entirely locked out of the interface. Active orders on the spot/futures books are canceled automatically, and API access is revoked.

### Unfreezing Protocol
To lift a security or compliance freeze, the account owner must complete our Enhanced Identity Verification process. This requires opening a ticket through support@apexcrypto.com and providing a high-resolution video showing the user holding their government ID alongside a handwritten note stating "ApexCrypto Unlock" and the current date. Legal and regulatory freezes can only be lifted upon official clearance from the instructing judicial entity.
