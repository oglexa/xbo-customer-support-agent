# XBO Help Center: Withdrawal Limits Framework

XBO establishes daily and monthly withdrawal ceilings to safeguard user funds and maintain regulatory alignment. These thresholds are strictly tied to the user's verified identity tier and the type of asset being moved (digital tokens or fiat currency).

### Standard Limits by Verification Tier

* **Tier 1 (Basic):** * **Crypto Withdrawals:** Disallowed. Users can deposit and trade, but cannot withdraw assets until Tier 2 status is achieved.
  * **Fiat Withdrawals:** Strictly unavailable.
* **Tier 2 (Advanced):**
  * **Crypto Withdrawals:** Maximum of $50,000 USD equivalent per rolling 24-hour window. Cumulative monthly limit is capped at $500,000 USD equivalent.
  * **Fiat Withdrawals:** Up to $10,000 USD (or local currency equivalent) per day via approved banking channels.
* **Tier 3 (Pro):**
  * **Crypto Withdrawals:** Maximum of $2,000,000 USD equivalent per rolling 24-hour window. Monthly crypto limits are entirely uncapped for Tier 3 accounts.
  * **Fiat Withdrawals:** Up to $100,000 USD per day, with a maximum monthly volume of $1,000,000 USD.

### Rolling Window Mechanism
All limits operate on a strict rolling 24-hour and 30-day basis, rather than resetting at midnight UTC. For example, if a Tier 2 user withdraws $30,000 USD worth of Bitcoin at 14:00 UTC, they can only withdraw an additional $20,000 USD worth of assets until 14:00 UTC the following day.

### Temporary Restrictions and Institutional Adjustments
* **New Security Credentials:** If an account updates its credentials (such as resetting a password, disabling 2FA, or updating a verified email address), all withdrawal privileges are frozen automatically for 48 hours for security reasons.
* **Custom Limits:** High-volume traders or institutional clients requiring limits exceeding Tier 3 baselines must undergo an enhanced due diligence (EDD) process to obtain custom structured parameters.
