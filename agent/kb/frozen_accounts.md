# ApexCrypto Help Center: User Guide for Restricted and Frozen Accounts

If you log into your ApexCrypto profile and encounter an "Account Restricted," "Suspended," or "Frozen" status banner, your access to certain or all platform functionalities has been temporarily decoupled by our Risk Management automated loops or Compliance staff. This guide outlines the exact tactical steps required to appeal the status and recover account access.

### Identifying Your Restriction Subtype

Before contacting support, determine the severity of your restriction by checking your dashboard error codes:

* **Status Code: SEC-LOCK-48:** This is a temporary 48-hour automated lock triggered by a recent security credential modification (e.g., password reset or 2FA rotation). *Resolution:* Do not open a support ticket. This lock is non-negotiable and resolves automatically exactly 48 hours from the timestamp of the modification.
* **Status Code: COMP-RISK-VEL:** Your account has triggered a velocity anomaly flag (cross-referenced in *Suspicious Activity Monitoring*). Outbound transfers are locked. *Resolution:* Check your registered email for an encrypted message from security@apexcrypto.com and follow the video verification prompt immediately.
* **Status Code: LEGAL-MAND-00:** The account is fully frozen due to a regulatory, judicial, or law enforcement directive. *Resolution:* Customer support agents cannot provide details, transaction logs, or asset balances for accounts marked with this code. You must await direct contact from our Legal Affairs team.

### Step-by-Step Appeal and Resolution Protocol

To expedite the manual review of a compliance-initiated freeze, follow these exact formatting instructions:

1. **Initiate the Case:** Open a single support ticket via the Helpdesk interface or email support@apexcrypto.com using your registered email address. Subject line format must be: `[Freeze Appeal] Account ID: [Your 9-Digit UID]`.
2. **Submit Video Identity Verification (VIV):** Attach a high-definition, unedited video clip (MP4 format, under 50MB) displaying the following:
   * Your face clearly illuminated with no glasses, hats, or heavy shadows.
   * You holding your registered government-issued photo ID next to your face.
   * You speaking clearly into the camera, reciting the exact mandatory phrase: *"I am [Full Legal Name], authorizing the security review of my ApexCrypto account UID [UID Number] on this day [Current Date]. I confirm all recent transactions were executed by me."*
3. **Provide Supporting Transaction Ledger:** If your freeze was triggered by an incoming transaction from a high-risk counterparty, attach a full-window screenshot of the originating external wallet or platform showing the TXID (Transaction Hash), date, and amount.

### Review Turnaround and SLA Timelines
Standard compliance appeals are processed chronologically. The baseline review window for a `COMP-RISK-VEL` flag is 3 to 5 business days after the submission of a valid Video Identity Verification. Submitting multiple tickets for the same freeze will reset your position in the compliance queue and delay processing.
